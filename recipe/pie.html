Grandma's Lemon Meringue Pie

  • One 9 inch pie
  • 30 Min - Pre time
  • 10 Min -Cook time
  • 40 Min total
  • 8 Servings

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 cup with sugar
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 3 tbsp cornstarch
  • 1/4 tbsb salt
  • 1/2 cups water
  • 2 lemons, juiced and zested
  • 2 tbsp butterr
  • 4 egg yolks, beaten
  • 1 (9 inch) pie crust, baked
  • 4 egg whites
  • 6 tbsp white suqar

DIRECTIONS

  1. PreheatOven:Preheat oven to 350 degree F(175 degree C)
  2. Make Lemon Filling : In medium saucepan..
    • Whisk together 1 cup sugar, flour, cornstarch, and salt.
    • Stir in water, lemon juice and lemon zest.
    • Cook over medium-high heat, stirring frequently, until mixture comes to a boil.
    • Stir in butter.
    • Place egg yolks in a small bowl and gradually whisk in 1/2 cup of hot sugar mixture.
    • Whisk egg yolk mixture back into remaining sugar mixture.
    • Bring to a boil and continue to cook while stirring constantly until thick.
    • Remove from heat.
    • Pour filling into baked pastry shell.
  3. Make Meringue:In a large glass or metal
    • Whip egg whites until foamy.
    • Add sugar gradually,and continue to whip until staff peacks foam.
    • Spread meringue over pie ,sealing the edges at the crust.
  4. Bake: Bake in preheated oven for 10 minuts,or until meringue is golden brown.

This is our favorite recipe here at Granny's Pies. It has been enjoyed by pie fans for many years. It's Granny's favorite! We hope you'll find that this recipe is delicious and also easy to follow; it's a piece of cake pie!
